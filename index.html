Fee model: Tiered per loan sequence (see Section 3). On-time fee if paid by DPD 3; automatic surcharge after DPD 3 (see Section 4).

Interest: 8.5% per month = 0.283% daily. Applied on principal for 30 days.

IGV (VAT): 18% levied on all revenue components (administration fee, surcharge, extension fee, penalty fees). Not part of APR/TCEA calculation.

Interest during delay: After the due date, regular interest (8.5%/mo) and penalty interest (~1%/mo) accrue simultaneously on the outstanding principal. No additional interest is charged during an extension period.

Disbursement: Full principal disbursed to client’s bank account (CCI). Fees collected at repayment.

Currency: PEN / Soles (S/)

4. Reversed-Penalty Pricing

How it works

The customer sees two prices tied to two dates:

  • “If you repay by [due date + 3 days], your administration fee is X.”
  • “If you plan to repay after that date, the price is Y.”

The higher price applies automatically from DPD 4 onward (a one-time surcharge added to the administration fee). If the customer takes an extension, the surcharge is cancelled — even if already triggered. Once an extension is purchased, the surcharge does not re-apply for the remainder of that loan.

Example — Maria, Seq 2 (Fresh), S/300, due 23 June:
Admin fee (35%) = S/105. Surcharge (40%) = S/120. She sees:
“Repay by 26 June: administration fee S/105” / “Repay after 26 June: administration fee S/225”

• Pays 22 June (before due): admin fee S/105. Done.
• Pays 25 June (3 days after due): admin fee S/105. Done.
• Pays 27 June (4 days after due): admin fee S/225. Surcharge added automatically on day 4.
• Extension on 22 June: pays S/90 ext fee. No surcharge. New due: 23 July.
• Extension on 27 June: pays S/90 ext fee. Surcharge cancelled.

5. Extension Pricing

Extensions are priced as a percentage of principal, scaling automatically across all tiers. Subject to 18% IGV.

  • 30-day extension: 30% of principal
  • 14-day extension: 19% of principal
  • 7-day extension: 12% of principal

Extension adds days to the original due date, not from the date the extension is taken. Extension application valid for 3 days. Taking an extension cancels any triggered surcharge.

6. Calculator & TCEA / TCEM

Formulas

Interest = Principal × 8.5% / 30 × term days

Fee = Principal × tier fee %

IGV = 18% × (Interest + Fee) — applied to all revenue components (admin fee, surcharge, extension fee, penalty fees, interest)

Total to repay = Principal + Interest + Fee + IGV

TCEM = (Principal + Interest + Fee) / Principal − 1 (excludes IGV)

TCEA = (1 + TCEM)12 − 1

STEP 6 / 6
Verification (e-KYC)

Trigger: Step 5 processing passed. Provider: Truora.

  • Preparation screen: instructions (have ID ready, good lighting, working camera, stable internet).
  • Verification process: embedded Truora flow — facial matching (selfie vs. ID), document authentication, OCR extraction (DOB, gender, name, ID number), cross-check against government registries.
  • Post-verification processing: final assessment with real-time API calls (Equifax/Infocorp, scoring). Up to 120 seconds.
  • Outcomes:
    • Preliminary approval → manual review pending (first 6 months: 100% manual verification).
    • Rejection → neutral message, no specific reason disclosed, “Log out” button only.
  • After approval: Offer presentation with final loan terms → SMS contract signature → disbursement via Kushki.
RED FLAG (from RegForm Summary): Verification retry option “currently not available but is a MUST.” Client must be able to retry failed verification without restarting the entire flow.

POST-JOURNEY
Customer Zone & Loan Extension

After disbursement, the client accesses their Customer Zone.

  • Active loan overview: Loan ID, amount, total to pay (on-time + late prices), commissions, disbursed amount, penalty/day, term, due date, countdown.
  • Pay loan → Kushki payment interface.
  • Extend loan → dropdown (7d / 14d / 30d), dynamic fee + new due date, redirect to Kushki for extension payment.
  • Previous loans history — read-only list.
  • Apply for new loan — blocked if active loan exists. Slider min 100, max from risk rules, default = max, step 50.
